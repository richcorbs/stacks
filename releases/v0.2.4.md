## Bug Fixes

- **Fixed garbled Unicode characters in terminal output**: Multi-byte UTF-8 characters (box-drawing, arrows, symbols) could be split across PTY read boundaries, causing replacement character ("?") glyphs. Now detects incomplete UTF-8 sequences and reassembles them across reads.
- **Set UTF-8 locale in PTY child process**: Ensures `LANG` and `LC_CTYPE` default to `en_US.UTF-8` so programs output valid UTF-8.
- **Complete box drawing coverage**: Added all remaining characters in U+2500-U+257F (half-lines, dashed variants, mixed heavy/light).
