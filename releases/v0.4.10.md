## Stacks v0.4.10

### Bug Fixes

- **Kill the full terminal process group on pane/session close** — deleting a terminal, closing a pane, or tearing down a session now terminates all jobs started inside that terminal session instead of only signalling the shell process.
- **Prevent orphaned background servers after terminal deletion** — fixes cases where long-running child processes such as Rails servers could survive terminal deletion and continue holding ports open.
